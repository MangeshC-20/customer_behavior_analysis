# customer_behavior_analysis
This project analyzed 3,900 customer purchases to understand shopping patterns, product preferences, and subscription behavior. The goal was to generate insights to guide strategic business decisions using python, SQL and power BI.
Customer Shopping Behavior AnalysisThis project executes a full-stack data analysis workflow on 3,900 customer purchase records to derive actionable business intelligence.
The process involved data cleaning and feature engineering in Python, structured querying in PostgreSQL (SQL), and visualization in Power BI.
Key Insights & FindingsRevenue Leaders: Male customers generated significantly higher total revenue ($157,890) than Female customers ($75,191).
The Young Adult age group contributed the most total revenue.
Subscription Status: The majority of customers (73%) do not have a subscription.
Product Performance: Clothing is the leading category in both revenue and sales. The product with the highest average rating is Gloves (3.86).High Spenders: 839 customers used a discount but still spent above the average purchase amount.
